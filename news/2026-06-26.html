4. OpenAI and Broadcom Debut "Jalapeno" - First Custom AI Inference Chip

OpenAI unveiled its first custom AI chip, "Jalapeno," built with Broadcom and Celestica specifically for large-scale inference workloads. The chip measures roughly 840mm2 in die size (near the EUV reticle limit) and was developed in a nine-month cycle. OpenAI plans to use enough homegrown chips to consume ten gigawatts of electricity, a direct strategic move to reduce dependence on Nvidia.
