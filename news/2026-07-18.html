[US_FLAG] Mira Murati's Thinking Machines Lab Ships Inkling, the Largest US Open-Weight Model

Former OpenAI CTO Mira Murati's Thinking Machines Lab released Inkling, a 975-billion-parameter Apache 2.0 multimodal model supporting text, image, and audio inputs with a 1-million-token context window. It scored 97.1% on the AIME 2026 benchmark and is positioned as a US-developed open-weight alternative built for enterprise fine-tuning rather than benchmark-chasing.
