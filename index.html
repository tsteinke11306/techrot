About

TechRot is a channel dedicated to cutting through the hype of the AI industry. We compare models head-to-head, test them on real workloads, and report on the news that actually matters.

Every week we drop a news episode covering the latest in AI — releases, drama, research, and what it all means. When a new model lands, we put out a comparison or deep-dive test on its own schedule. No filler, no calendar padding — just analysis when there's something worth analyzing.

No sponsorships. No hype trains. Just honest comparisons and the AI news you need to make your own decisions.
